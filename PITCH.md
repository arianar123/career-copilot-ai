# 30-Second Pitch

CareerCopilot AI is a student-focused career intelligence workspace. Instead of acting like just another chatbot, it helps users analyze their resume against target roles, understand which skills the market rewards, practice interviews with feedback, and track progress over time in one connected system. The core idea is to make career prep feel less like guesswork and more like a guided, data-driven workflow.

# 60-Second Pitch

Most students use separate tools for resume editing, job research, interview practice, and tracking applications. CareerCopilot AI brings those workflows together into one experience. A user can upload a resume, compare it against a target role, get missing skills and stronger bullet suggestions, see market intelligence for that role, practice an interview with coaching feedback, and track progress in a dashboard. The project stands out because it is not just answering questions. It is coordinating a career-prep system around real outcomes.

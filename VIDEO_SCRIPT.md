# Video Script

## 60-90 Second Recording Script

Hi, this is CareerCopilot AI, an AI-powered career intelligence workspace designed for students.

The core problem is that career prep is usually fragmented. Students use one tool for resumes, another for interview practice, another for job research, and nothing really ties the workflow together.

CareerCopilot AI brings those pieces into one system.

First, I can open the resume analysis flow, load demo data, and generate a report. The app gives me a match-style score, missing skills, rewritten bullets, and a 30 / 60 / 90 day roadmap.

Next, I can open the market intelligence page to see what skills, salary signals, and hiring patterns matter most for a role in a specific region.

Then I can go to the dashboard to review saved analyses and trends over time.

After that, I can open the mock interview coach, generate tailored questions, and get feedback on confidence, clarity, technical depth, and ways to improve my answer.

Finally, the workspace page lets the user personalize the system with their target role, region, and career goals so the product feels personal instead of generic.

CareerCopilot AI is built with Next.js, FastAPI, and a persistent backend structure, and it is designed to act less like a chatbot and more like a personal career operating system.

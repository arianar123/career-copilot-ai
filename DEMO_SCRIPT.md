# Demo Script

## Goal

Show that CareerCopilot AI is a complete career-prep workflow, not just a single AI feature.

## Before You Start

- Run `.\start-local.ps1`
- Open `http://127.0.0.1:3000`
- Keep `http://127.0.0.1:8000/docs` available as a backup proof point

## 90-Second Flow

1. Homepage
   Explain that the product combines resume analysis, market intelligence, interview coaching, and progress tracking.

2. Resume Analysis
   Open `/upload`, click `Load demo data`, and generate a report.
   Mention:
   - match score
   - missing skills
   - rewritten bullets
   - roadmap

3. Market Intelligence
   Open `/market`, load the demo market, and generate a snapshot.
   Mention:
   - demand level
   - salary range
   - top skills

4. Dashboard
   Open `/dashboard` and show saved analyses plus trend cards.

5. Interview Coach
   Open `/interview`, load demo data, generate questions, and request feedback.
   Mention:
   - score breakdown
   - improvements
   - stronger answer

6. Workspace
   Open `/workspace` and explain that the app stores role, region, and goal defaults to personalize the system.

## Closing Line

CareerCopilot AI is designed to act less like a chatbot and more like a personal career operating system for students.
